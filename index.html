The Problem

India's "Xerox Shop" is a Privacy Crisis

💻
Data Stays Behind
Files remain in "Downloads" and "Recent Documents" on public PCs for days — your Aadhaar, property papers, legal documents exposed to anyone.
👀
Operator Access
Sensitive documents are handled by operators who may intentionally or unintentionally view, store, or share your private information.
⏱️
Operational Friction
Long queues, limited digital payment options in smaller towns, and restricted hours create daily frustration for millions of users.
The Solution

Everything India Needs
in One Kiosk

🛡️
DPDP Act Compliant
Bilingual consent screens, automated data erasure, and a Certificate of Erasure via WhatsApp/SMS after every session. Your strongest legal moat.
🏛️
Government-in-a-Box
E-stamping via SHCIL, DigiLocker document fetch via OTP, and pre-loaded government forms — Passport, PAN, Voter ID, Ration Card.
💸
UPI-First Payments
Dynamic QR codes for PhonePe, GPay, and Paytm. Micro-transactions as low as ₹2 per page. Student Packs for a full semester.
⚡
Built for Bharat
Integrated UPS for power cuts, dust-proof and heat-resistant casing for non-AC environments, and 500+ sheet capacity trays.
💬
WhatsApp-to-Print
With 500M+ Indian users on WhatsApp, send any PDF to a dedicated SecurePrint number and receive a Print Code instantly. Walk to the nearest kiosk, enter the code, print — done. No email logins. No USB drives. No waiting.
